AgenticCommerce — Encrypted Deliverable Proof Primitive

Hackathon demo: make proofHash = sha256(ciphertext) concrete, reproducible, and reviewable.

30–60s Quickstart

cd ..
python3 scripts/encrypted_bundle.py
cat demo/encrypted-bundle.sample.json

This produces a demo encrypted bundle file and prints: plaintext_sha256, ciphertext_sha256 (the on-chain proofHash), and decrypt_ok.

Security note: this demo uses XOR to avoid dependencies. Replace with AES-256-GCM in production.

Why this matters

  • Public chain gets a verifiable receipt (sha256(ciphertext)) without leaking deliverables.
  • Buyer verifies off-chain by decrypting and recomputing sha256(plaintext).
  • On dispute, re-encrypt for arbiters or reveal under a bond while keeping an auditable trail.
